About Kumon Fishermall QC

Kumon Fishermall QC is committed to developing independent, confident learners through the globally proven Kumon Method. Our center provides a structured yet supportive environment where students build strong academic foundations in Mathematics and Reading.

We believe that every child has the potential to excel. Through consistent daily practice and individualized learning, students gradually develop focus, discipline, and the ability to solve problems independently.

Our instructors carefully guide each student to ensure steady progress while encouraging self-learning and long-term academic growth.

Our Center Philosophy

  • ✔ Strengthen academic fundamentals
  • ✔ Build confidence through mastery
  • ✔ Encourage discipline and consistency

The Kumon Method

The Kumon Method is a globally recognized learning system that focuses on individualized instruction and self-learning. Each student begins at a level where they can succeed independently, building confidence before gradually progressing to more advanced material.

Rather than simply teaching for short-term exam performance, Kumon develops long-term academic ability by strengthening concentration, time management, and perseverance — essential skills for success in school and beyond.

Kumon Connect

Kumon Connect is Kumon’s digital learning platform that allows students to complete their worksheets on a tablet while maintaining the same individualized learning method and academic standards as traditional paper worksheets.

Digital Learning (Kumon Connect)

  • ✔ Worksheets completed on tablet with stylus
  • ✔ Instant submission and faster instructor feedback
  • ✔ Progress tracking and performance monitoring
  • ✔ No need to carry physical worksheets
  • ✔ Secure cloud-based storage of completed work
  • ✔ Convenient for both in-center and home study

Traditional Paper Worksheets

  • ✔ Familiar and structured learning format
  • ✔ Strong handwriting and fine motor skill development
  • ✔ Physical tracking of completed worksheets
  • ✔ Minimal screen exposure
  • ✔ Proven method used globally for over 60 years
  • ✔ Ideal for students who prefer tactile learning

One Method, Two Learning Options

Whether through paper worksheets or Kumon Connect digital tablets, students follow the same carefully structured Kumon Method. Both formats build independence, confidence, and strong academic foundations. Parents may choose the format that best suits their child’s learning style.
