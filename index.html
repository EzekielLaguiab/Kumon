The Kumon Method

A proven, self-paced learning system used by millions of students across 50+ countries.

The Kumon Method is a globally recognized learning system that focuses on individualized instruction and self-learning. Each student begins at a level where they can succeed independently, building confidence before gradually progressing to more advanced material.

Rather than simply teaching for short-term exam performance, Kumon develops long-term academic ability by strengthening concentration, time management, and perseverance — essential skills for success in school and beyond.

Founded in Japan in 1958 by Toru Kumon, the method was originally created by a father who wanted to help his own son excel in mathematics. Today, Kumon operates in over 50 countries and has helped more than 4 million students reach their full potential.

📐

Individualized Instruction

Every student has a unique starting point and progresses at their own pace — never rushed, never held back.

🔁

Repetition & Mastery

Concepts are repeated and refined until full mastery is achieved, ensuring no gaps in understanding before advancing.

🧠

Self-Learning

Worksheets are designed so students learn by doing — developing the ability to think and solve independently.

Kumon Connect

Kumon's digital learning platform — same trusted method, now available on tablet.

Kumon Connect is Kumon's official digital learning platform that allows students to complete their worksheets on a tablet using a stylus, while maintaining the same individualized learning method and academic standards as traditional paper worksheets.

Designed for modern learners, Kumon Connect seamlessly integrates technology into the Kumon experience — giving instructors real-time insights into student performance and allowing for faster, more precise feedback without ever compromising the integrity of the Kumon Method.

💻

Digital Learning (Kumon Connect)

  • ✔ Worksheets completed on tablet with stylus
  • ✔ Instant submission and faster instructor feedback
  • ✔ Real-time progress tracking & performance monitoring
  • ✔ No need to carry physical worksheets
  • ✔ Secure cloud-based storage of all completed work
  • ✔ Convenient for both in-center and home study
  • ✔ Eco-friendly — paperless learning environment
